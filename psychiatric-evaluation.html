Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a psychiatric evaluation take?
Initial psychiatric evaluations at Jupco are typically 90 minutes. This allows sufficient time for a thorough clinical interview, assessment, and initial treatment planning without feeling rushed.
Do I need a referral from my primary care doctor?
No referral is required to schedule a psychiatric evaluation at Jupco. You can contact us directly to book an appointment. However, we may request records from your other providers to ensure coordinated care.
What happens after the evaluation?
Following your evaluation, your psychiatrist will provide a diagnostic impression and discuss recommended treatment options. This may include therapy, medication management, lifestyle changes, or referrals to other specialists. You'll receive a written summary and treatment plan.
Is the evaluation confidential?
Absolutely. All psychiatric evaluations are protected by strict confidentiality laws (HIPAA). Your information is never shared without your written consent, except in rare emergency situations as required by law.
Do you offer virtual evaluations?
Yes, we offer secure telehealth psychiatric evaluations for patients who prefer virtual appointments. The same comprehensive 90-minute assessment is conducted via our HIPAA-compliant video platform.
