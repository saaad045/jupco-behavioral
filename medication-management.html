Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How often do I need to see my psychiatrist for medication management?
Initially, appointments are typically every 2-4 weeks to monitor response and adjust dosages. Once stable, follow-ups may be every 1-3 months. Telehealth options are available for many follow-up appointments.
Do I need therapy in addition to medication?
For many conditions, combining medication with therapy provides the best outcomes. We can provide referrals to therapists or coordinate with your existing therapist for integrated care.
How long until I feel better on medication?
Response varies by medication and individual. Some notice improvement within 1-2 weeks, while others may take 4-8 weeks to experience full benefits. We monitor closely and adjust as needed.
Do you offer telehealth medication management?
Yes, we offer secure video appointments for medication management follow-ups. Initial evaluations may be conducted in-person or via telehealth depending on your needs and state regulations.
Can I get my prescriptions filled at my regular pharmacy?
Yes. We send electronic prescriptions directly to your preferred pharmacy. We also offer prior authorization assistance when needed for insurance coverage.
