Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know which service is right for me?
We offer a free 15-minute consultation to discuss your needs and recommend the most appropriate service. You'll speak with a care coordinator who will help match you with the right specialist and treatment approach.
Do you accept insurance?
Yes, we accept most major insurance plans including Blue Cross, Aetna, Cigna, and UnitedHealthcare. We also offer flexible payment plans and sliding scale fees for those without insurance.
How quickly can I get an appointment?
New patient appointments are typically available within 3-5 business days. For urgent needs, we offer priority scheduling within 24-48 hours.
Are virtual sessions as effective as in-person?
Research shows virtual therapy is equally effective for most conditions. We offer both options, and many patients prefer the convenience and privacy of our secure telehealth platform.
What's your cancellation policy?
We require 24 hours notice for cancellations. Late cancellations or no-shows may incur a fee equal to 50% of the session cost. Exceptions made for emergencies.
Is my information confidential?
Absolutely. We comply with all HIPAA regulations and use encrypted platforms for all communications. Your privacy is our highest priority.
