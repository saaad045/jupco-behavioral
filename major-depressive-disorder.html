Understanding MDD

Recognizing Major Depressive Disorder

MDD is more than sadness — it's a persistent medical condition that affects every aspect of life.

Emotional Symptoms

Persistent sadness, emptiness, hopelessness, loss of interest in activities once enjoyed, irritability, and feelings of worthlessness.

Cognitive Symptoms

Difficulty concentrating, indecisiveness, memory problems, negative thought patterns, and recurrent thoughts of death or suicide.

Physical Symptoms

Changes in sleep (insomnia or oversleeping), appetite/weight changes, fatigue, low energy, and unexplained aches or pains.

Evidence-Based Care

Comprehensive Depression Treatment

We combine multiple evidence-based approaches to address depression from every angle.

Medication Management

Expert prescribing of antidepressants (SSRIs, SNRIs, etc.) with careful monitoring for effectiveness and side effects.

Psychotherapy

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) and other evidence-based modalities to change negative thought patterns and behaviors.

Holistic Support

Lifestyle interventions including sleep hygiene, exercise guidance, nutrition support, and stress reduction techniques.
