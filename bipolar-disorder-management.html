Understanding Bipolar

What Is Bipolar Disorder?

Bipolar disorder is a brain disorder characterized by extreme mood shifts between mania/hypomania and depression.

Manic / Hypomanic Episodes

Periods of elevated, expansive, or irritable mood with increased energy and activity.

  • Decreased need for sleep
  • Racing thoughts, rapid speech
  • Grandiose plans or beliefs
  • Impulsive or risky behavior
  • Increased goal-directed activity

Depressive Episodes

Periods of low mood, loss of interest, and reduced energy lasting at least two weeks.

  • Persistent sadness or emptiness
  • Loss of interest in activities
  • Fatigue or low energy
  • Changes in sleep or appetite
  • Thoughts of death or suicide
Evidence-Based Care

Our Comprehensive Approach to Bipolar Management

Effective bipolar treatment requires a multi-faceted strategy. We address every aspect of your well-being.

Medication Management

Expert prescribing of mood stabilizers and other medications. Careful monitoring for effectiveness and side effects.

Psychotherapy

CBT, interpersonal therapy, and psychoeducation to help recognize triggers, manage symptoms, and prevent relapse.

Mood Monitoring

Tracking mood patterns, sleep, and energy levels to identify early warning signs of episode shifts.

Lifestyle Medicine

Supporting Stability Through Healthy Habits

Sleep Hygiene

Consistent sleep schedules are essential for mood stability in bipolar disorder.

Routine Management

Regular daily routines help reduce triggers for mood episodes.

Stress Reduction

Mindfulness and stress management techniques to prevent episode triggers.

Support Systems

Family education and peer support for long-term recovery.

Stability Is Possible

With proper treatment and support, individuals with bipolar disorder can live full, productive lives. Let us help you find the right path.
